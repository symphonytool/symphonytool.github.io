The Symphony IDE

Formal Modelling for Systems of Systems

Welcome to The Symphony IDE.

The Symphony IDE is an open-source tool originally developed in the EU (COMPASS (Comprehensive Modelling for Advanced Systems of Systems))[http://www.compass-research.eu/] research project. The focus is on modelling systems of systems. The IDE is based on the (Overture platform)[http://www.overturetool.org/].

Getting the tool.

Getting the code.

All of the Symphony code is hosted on GitHub, under the @symphonytool organisation account. Everyone is welcome to grab it, fork it, and contribute.
